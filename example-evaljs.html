Peek42 - eval JS [Index]

Tap the input box. Press enter to evaluate the pre-filled expression. Prefix with one of v(alue) (the default), p(retty), t(ype), d(esc) (requires two arguments separated by a comma), m(embers), c(hain), a(pi) to see different representations. The input box focus is preserved, so hitting enter after tapping Clear evaluates the expression again
